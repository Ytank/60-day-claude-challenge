How to Play

You're managing a live global supply chain. Alerts appear on the dashboard describing a real operational problem. You have limited time per alert and limited time overall (3 minutes).

Your job

  • Read each alert's priority, description, and business impact.
  • Pick the action that best fits the situation — the right fix protects revenue and KPIs; the wrong one wastes money and trust.
  • Ignore is sometimes fine for low-stakes noise, but ignoring a real problem tanks Service Level and Customer Satisfaction.
  • Delay Decision buys time but the issue often gets worse before it resolves.
  • Unattended alerts expire on their own — usually badly.

KPIs

Service Level, Customer Satisfaction, Inventory Health, and Transportation Efficiency are percentages you want to protect. Operating Cost climbs with every action — cheap, well-matched fixes keep it under control. Revenue Protected and Score go up with sound calls.
