How this works

This is not a resume reviewer — it's an interview stress-test. It reads your resume, pulls out individual claims (achievements, ownership statements, metrics), and interviews you on each one, one at a time.

Why it gets tougher

The interviewer persona starts warm and curious. If your answers stay vague or lack specifics, the follow-up questions get more pointed — exactly like a real senior interviewer probing for substance.

Confidence scoring

After each answer, your confidence score for that claim updates based on specificity, evidence, and how well you handled the follow-up — not on how long your answer was.

Defense Report

At the end, you get a full readiness breakdown: which claims are well-defended, which are weak, and concrete prep recommendations for each one before you face a real interviewer.

Your data

Everything is stored in this browser's local storage only. Nothing is uploaded anywhere except to Claude for generating questions and evaluations during your session.
