Summary

This is the notice for the 81st Annual General Meeting of Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Limited (formerly Tata Motors Limited), to be held entirely via video conferencing on Wednesday, July 8, 2026. Members will vote on 9 items: 4 ordinary business items (financial statements, dividend, and the rotational re-appointment of Mr. N Chandrasekaran) and 5 special business items (re-appointment of Independent Director Mr. Al-Noor Ramji, branch auditor authorization, cost auditor remuneration ratification, and two material related-party-transaction approvals).

Because the AGM is VC/OAVM-only, the usual proxy facility is not available — individual Members must either e-vote remotely or attend and vote live. The notice also bundles a long set of administrative deadlines (email registration, TDS forms, speaker registration, e-voting window) that carry real cut-off dates.

⚠️ Today is Monday, June 22, 2026. The deadline to register an unregistered e-mail address with the RTA (to receive the Notice and e-voting credentials) is 5:00 PM IST today — see Action Items below.

Risks / Blockers 5
🔴 Invalid or inoperative PAN
If PAN is not correct, invalid, or inoperative, tax on the dividend will be deducted at a higher rate and TDS credit will not be available (Section 397 of the IT Act).
🔴 KYC non-compliance blocks dividend payout (physical holders)
Folios must be KYC compliant to receive dividends electronically; required documents (Form ISR-1, cancelled cheque, self-attested PAN/address proof, Form SH-13/ISR-3) must reach the RTA by the record date.
🟠 No proxy facility for this AGM
Because the meeting is VC/OAVM-only, individual Members cannot appoint a proxy. Only institutional/corporate Members can appoint an authorized representative under Sections 112/113.
🟠 E-voting login lockout
The NSDL e-voting login disables after five unsuccessful password attempts, requiring a "Forgot Password" reset before voting can proceed.
🟢 Dormant demat accounts
Members are advised not to leave demat accounts dormant for long, to reduce fraud risk and keep holdings statements current.
Conflicts 2
⚠️ Typo in dividend payment date
The Notice states the dividend will be paid "on before Tuseday, July 14, 2026" — "Tuseday" appears to be a typo for "Tuesday" in the source document, reproduced here exactly as written.
⚠️ Record-date-linked deadlines fall before today's date
The dividend record date (Friday, June 19, 2026) and the KYC/Form ISR-1 submission deadline tied to it have already passed as of today, June 22, 2026 — yet the e-mail registration deadline elsewhere in the same Notice is set for today, June 22, 2026. The Notice doesn't reconcile this; Members should not assume the record-date items are still open.
